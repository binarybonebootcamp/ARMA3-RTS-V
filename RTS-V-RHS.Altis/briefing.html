RTS-V HYBRID

Click STATUS MENU in-game and click HELP in the upper right corner of the STATUS MENU for rules.

Simplified Objectives:

Build a base anywhere using the MCU (Mobile Construction Unit).

Capture territories for resources capacity and credits.

Use resources and/or credits, build an army, capture your enemy's resources, and destroy their base.

GAME MODES:

Victory Point Control - Control more victory points than the other team to make their victory point ticker reach 0.

Annihilation - If your opponent controls less than three territories, destroy the enemy base to win.

ECONOMY TYPES:

Resource Capacity - Your team must have enough of certain resources to create units.

Resource Credits - You use credits to purchase units.

Hybrid - You use credits and resource capacity for building units.
